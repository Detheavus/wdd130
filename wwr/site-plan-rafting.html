Overview

Purpose

There are several purposes of this web page. One is to inform the public about this business, another is to educate about white water rafting. An additional purpose is to help the business by allowing online purchase of rafting trips.

Audience

The primary audience of this page are people with an interest in whitewater rafting. What do they want? The audience wants information about the river(s) that can be traversed with the guides of this company. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? The audience will expect to find all information relevant to the river(s), conditions of those river(s) at a given time/season, the costs associated with the rafting trip, experience of the business staff/river guides, tangential activities that can be had with the rafting trip, costs of activities. How will my audience access my site? Most people will probably access the site from a mobile device, but the site will be responsive and accommodate desktop/laptop and tablet browsers.
